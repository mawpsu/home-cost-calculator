CT Home Cost Calculator

GitHub Pages–ready calculator for townhouse vs. single-family homes in central Connecticut. Type exact square footage, estimate monthly payment from price/down payment/rate, and review the calculation logic in a separate tab.

Home setup

For townhouses, lawn care and snow removal are assumed covered by HOA. For single-family homes, they are broken out separately.

Mortgage & ownership inputs

Loan term is fixed at 30 years. PMI is included only when down payment is less than 20%.
